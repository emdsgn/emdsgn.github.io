GiraffeBaby

GiraffeBabyTM is a baby brand for innovative product made up of PLA .When we started families of our own, we shared a mission: to help parents like us create a baby-safe world. We would do this by creating a world of products made to go wherever babies go. Because exploring and discovering is a baby’s full time job. Our job—every parent’s job— is to make it safe for babies to do theirs. It’s the best way to make sure they fully develop and thrive.

The goal of this project was to redesign and develop their website. The website was developed through Shopify with a unique template designed by me.
